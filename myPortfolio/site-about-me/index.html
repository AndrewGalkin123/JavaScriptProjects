Hi, I am Andrew!

Frontend developer




At the age of 13, I accidentally entered the world of information technology by completing online courses at the STEP Academy. A brief break in my learning after that didn't deter my passion for IT. Returning a year later, I joined Java courses at Hillel, immersing myself in object-oriented programming. However, the programming world proved to be boundless, and I delved into the study of JavaScript, which became the key to frontend development. Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I created interactive web pages and applications, and also mastered frameworks like Node.js, Express.js, and React.js. Continuously improving, I solved problems on LeetCode to enhance my programming skills. My passion for IT became my career as a Frontend Developer, and this is just the beginning of my journey.
