Telegram bots

I have developed two Telegram bots, each serving a specific purpose. The first bot is dedicated to helping users with mathematics. It's equipped with various math-related features, including solving equations, providing explanations for mathematical concepts, and offering step-by-step solutions to math problems. Users can interact with this bot to get instant assistance with their math-related queries, making it a handy tool for students and anyone in need of mathematical support.


The second bot is a food delivery bot that streamlines the process of ordering and receiving food through Telegram. Users can browse menus, place orders, specify delivery preferences, and make payments directly within the chat interface. This bot integrates with local restaurants and food delivery services, ensuring a seamless and convenient experience for customers looking to satisfy their culinary cravings without leaving the Telegram app.


Both bots demonstrate my skills in bot development and my ability to create practical solutions for different user needs, from academic assistance to enhancing the food delivery experience.
